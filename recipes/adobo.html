Adobo

Description

Pork Adobo is pork cooked in soy sauce, vinegar, and garlic. It is considered as the Philippine's national dish because of its popularity.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs pork belly
  • 2 tablespoons garlic
  • 5 dried bay leaves
  • 4 tablespoons vinegar
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon peppercorn
  • 2 cups water
  • Salt to taste

Steps in making the dish

  1. Combine the pork belly, soy sauce, and garlic then marinade for at least 1 hour
  2. Heat the pot and put-in the marinated pork belly. Cook this all up for a few minutes
  3. Add water, whole peppercorn, and dried bay leaves. Then bring your mixture to a boil. Simmer for 40 minutes to 1 hour
  4. Put the vinegar inside and simmer for 12 to 15 minutes
  5. Add salt to taste
  6. Serve hot. Share and enjoy!
